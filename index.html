Project Overview

Subject: Carbon Capture, Utilization and Storage

This interactive dashboard provides a micro-simulation of vehicular carbon dioxide emissions across two alternate localized routes on the IIT Hyderabad campus leading to the BTBM parking lot.

By actively comparing the Red Route (Workshop Loop) and the Violet Route (Academic Spine), the simulation demonstrates how optimized route selection, combined with granular traffic tracking, can directly minimize localized carbon footprints within a closed road network. Emission methodologies are bounded by rigorous WRI India 2015 uplift factors for accurate real-world analysis.
